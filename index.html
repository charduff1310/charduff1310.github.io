Welcome!

Welcome to the Chicago Field Hockey Club. We're glad you're here.

We are a friendly, local club which welcomes all ages and abilities for social and competitive hockey. We are a diverse group from all over the world.

Currently, the club runs a robust indoor league in the winter, plays in men's/women's and coed tournaments, and partners with the Northwestern Field Hockey Club for pickup hockey in the spring and summer.

Check out our membership page to see which CFHC offerings fit you best.
