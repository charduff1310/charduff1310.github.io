Membership

JOIN NOW

We welcome new members and look forward to connecting with you.

Please take care of the following items before coming out to play:

  • Join our facebook page to stay up to date on all news and events.
  • Register your contact information to receive tournament updates and sign-up polls.
  • Pay before you play and please include your name and email address for registration purposes.

There are three different ways to play: indoor (choose Season I, II, or both), outdoor season, and a combination of both. Alternatively, you can also play on a day pass for $20.
